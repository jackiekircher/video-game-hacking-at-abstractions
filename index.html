Cheat Engine is a really powerful program that attaches to a running process and let's you peek at its memory.

Here's a preview of Cheat Engine in action. On the left is the running game, and on the right are a some cheat engine windows. The top window is a radar that tracks enemy positions by polling map data, and the bottom is a live memory dump of the player.

Notice when the player moves these two values change, and when they collect items they're reflected in memory here.
